The Science

Whole body cryotherapy (WBC) works by stepping into a cryogenic chamber where liquid nitrogen is used to cool air down between -184°F and -292°F. Within moments, the body triggers the 'fight or flight' mechanism, but in a deeper and more sustained way. As your body temperature drops, vasoconstriction occurs, sending blood from peripheral tissues to the center of your body to protect the vital core organs with warmth. While in the central cardiovascular system, blood is supplied with oxygen and cleansed from toxins. When the participant exits the cryosauna, the newly enriched blood rushes back to the peripheral tissues warming the muscles immediately.

FAQ

1. What do I need to wear?
You don't need to wear anything specifically; we will give you everything you need. You will undress in a private dressing leaving only socks and underwear on. We will provide you with gloves, shoes, and a robe to wear. Once inside the cryo sauna, you will remove your robe. We will only see your face; the rest of your body will be concealed. We ask you to not have any moisture on you or on your clothes. No external metals like jewelry are allowed, except for metals inside the body like medical metal plates.

2. Is it painful?
Although it may sound very cold, it's not painful. In fact, it's a very tolerable experience since it's a completley dry treatment. You will not feel the cold pain as if you were in an ice bath. Safety is a top priority. A trained and certified REDUX technician will remain with you during the entire session.

3. How long will my first visit last?
You can expect your first visit to last around 12-15 minutes. Every visit thereafter will be around 5 minutes.

4. Do I have to do anything specifically after my session?
There is nothing specific that you should do or avoid. At the conclusion of your session your muscles will warm immediately after blood rushes back to your peripheral tissues. You can go on about your normal day.

5. How often should I do sessions?
It largely depends on your conditions, your goals, and lifestyle. You should undergo WBC on an as-needed basis. For performance-enhancing, you may want to use WBC in between 2 closely separated intense workouts. For recurring pains, as single session will bring you releif. For users interested in metabolism boosts, we recommend 2-3 sessions per week. For clients seeking the skin benefits, it will vary from client to client.
